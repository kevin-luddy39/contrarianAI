# contrarianAI — Positioning

## One-liner

We find what's actually wrong with your AI before your users do.

## Thesis

The AI industry has a quality gap. Organizations are shipping AI agents, copilots, and automation that look impressive in demos and fail in production. The failure modes are well-documented but widely ignored because they're unglamorous:

- Tool descriptions that misroute calls silently
- Context windows that degrade at 147K tokens, not 200K
- Agents that approve their own broken output
- 90% of "AI agents" that should be deterministic workflows
- Semantic ambiguity that makes AI give confident, plausible, wrong answers
- Memory systems that accumulate contradictions until they're useless

These aren't edge cases. They're the default outcome of building AI without structural engineering discipline.

## Target Clients

1. **Engineering teams shipping AI products** — Need architecture audits, tool configuration reviews, harness engineering guidance
2. **Enterprises deploying AI internally** — Need semantic layer audits, agent-vs-workflow assessments, production readiness scorecards
3. **Consultancies advising on AI** — Need frameworks, decision tools, and white-label audit methodologies

## Differentiation

- **Evidence-based:** Every service maps to documented failure modes from Anthropic's own research, production case studies, and benchmark data
- **Contrarian positioning:** We say the things vendors won't — that your agent should probably be a workflow, that a better model won't fix your architecture, that your AI is evaluating itself and lying about the results
- **Tooling + services:** Not just advice — diagnostic tools that measure the problems and track remediation
